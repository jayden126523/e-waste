E-Waste Awareness

What is E-Waste?

E-waste, or electronic waste, refers to discarded electronic devices and components. This includes items like old computers, mobile phones, televisions, and other electronic appliances.

Why is E-Waste a Problem?

E-waste contains hazardous materials that can harm the environment and human health if not disposed of properly. These materials include lead, mercury, and cadmium, which can contaminate soil and water.

How Can We Manage E-Waste?

Proper e-waste management involves recycling and reusing electronic devices. Many organizations offer e-waste recycling programs where you can safely dispose of your old electronics.
